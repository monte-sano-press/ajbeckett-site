About the Author

A.J. Beckett writes romance centered on the tension and intimacy that grow when two people are drawn together before they are ready to admit it.

The work explores chemistry, restraint, emotional risk, and the kinds of connection that quietly reshape a life.

A.J. Beckett lives and writes in Northern Alabama.
